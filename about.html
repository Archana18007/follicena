WHO WE ARE

Leading the Green Tableware Revolution

Welcome to Follicena, your life's green companion. With over two decades of experience, Follicena is a leading force in the tableware industry, dedicated to providing eco-friendly biodegradable dinner wares and food containers. Our commitment to sustainability has made us a household name, trusted for our high-quality, environmentally conscious products.

At Follicena, we operate state-of-the-art manufacturing plants that utilize cutting-edge technology to produce a wide range of biodegradable products that foster a green solution for your food storage and plating queries. From plates to containers, our products are designed to meet the ever-growing demand for eco-conscious dining options.

By choosing Follicena, you're not just choosing tableware – you're choosing a greener future. Join us in our mission to reduce environmental impact and make a positive difference for generations to come. Let Follicena be your partner in sustainable living.
